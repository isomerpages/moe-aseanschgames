<h5>In the name of all competitors<br>we promise that we shall take part<br>in these 14th ASEAN Schools Games,<br>respecting and abiding by the rules<br>which govern them,<br>committing ourselves to the true spirit of sportsmanship,<br>without doping and without drugs,<br>for the glory of sport<br>and the honour of our teams.</h5>
