<h4>In the name of all competitors</h4>
<h4>we promise that we shall take part</h4>
<h4>in these 13th ASEAN Schools Games,</h4>
<h4>respecting and abiding by the rules</h4>
<h4>which govern them,</h4>
<h4>committing ourselves to the true spirit of sportsmanship,</h4>
<h4>without doping and without drugs,</h4>
<h4>for the glory of sport</h4>
<h4>and the honour of our teams.</h4>
