<p>• The official logo was inspired by the national flower of Brunei Darussalam,
Simpur (Dillenia suffruticosa), locally known as Simpur Bini.</p>
<p>• The logo represents values, progress and connection to the natural environment.</p>
<p>• Jawi script in the logo reflects Brunei Darussalam as a country with
the concept of Malay Islamic Monarchy.</p>
<p>• The curve shape symbolises a dynamic journey of growth, progress and
success, represented by the three primary colour scheme of ASG14.</p>
